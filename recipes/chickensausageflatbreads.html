Chicken Sausage Flatbreads


Sourced from Hello Fresh

This chicken sausage flatbread recipe is a quick and easy way to prepare dinner. The base consists of tomato sauce, cheese, and sausage and is then topped with zucchini slices and fresh oregano.

Ingredients

  • Zucchini
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Flatbreads
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Oregano
  • Italian Chicken Sausage Mix
  • Colavita Crushed Tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F. Wash and dry produce.
  2. Trim zucchini into 1/2 inch thick rounds. Pick oregano leaves from stems and discard stems. Roughly chop leaves.
  3. Places zucchini on lightly oiled baking sheet and season with salt, pepper, and 1/2 tsp italian seasoning. Bake in oven (top rack) until browned and softened, 5-7 minutes. Remove sheet from oven, adjust temperature to 450 degrees F.
  4. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add sausage and half the oregano. Break up the meat and cook for 4-6 minutes. Turn off heat.
  5. Place flatbreads on a second lightly oiled baking sheet. Toast in oven until lightly browned, 5-7 minutes.
  6. Spread half the crushed tomatoes in a thin layer on toasted flatbreads. Season with salt, pepper, and 1/2 tsp italian seasoning. Top with sausage, zucchini, and mozzarella.
  7. Bake flatbreads until edges are golden brown and cheese has melted, 4-6 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle flatbreads with as much remaining oregano as preferred. Slice into pieces.
